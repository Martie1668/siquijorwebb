Portfolio

Siquijor Island is not just known for its stunning beaches and mystical charm but also for its growing art scene. Isla De Fuego Art Gallery showcases local artistry that highlights the island’s cultural heritage, while the Douglas Workshop Gallery in San Juan features unique wood carvings and paintings, including impressive ‘Easter Island’-like sculptures by Douglas Balwit. These galleries offer visitors a deeper appreciation of Siqui jor’s creative spirit, blending tradition with artistic expression in a serene island setting.

About

Social media in Siquijor plays a key role in community engagement, tourism promotion, and information sharing. Facebook is the most popular platform, with various groups focused on local news, tourism, community support, and entertainment. Notable groups include Siquijor Social Media, Discovering Siquijor, and the Siquijor International Community Group, where locals and visitors exchange information, services, and experiences. These platforms help foster connections and showcase the island's culture and attractions.
